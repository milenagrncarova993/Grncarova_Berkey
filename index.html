The Purification Process

The Black Berkey filters are designed to remove or dramatically reduce a broad range of contaminants.These contaminants go through a process called ionic "absorption".This method processes the water through micropores through the filters shell in convoluted twists know as the "tortuous path",further ensnaring the tiniest of single called organisms and pollutants.

Black Berkey Filters remove patogenic bacteria (and surrogets) to >99.9999% and viruses to >99.9999% (Log 5)
